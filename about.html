About

Drafts begins with a simple thought: not everything is meant to be sent.

Posts are anonymous. Nothing is measured or displayed,only the words, and the possibility that someone recognizes them.

Nothing here is meant to circulate Only to be released, recognized, and momentarily understood.

How it works

  • Write an unsent message and optional lingering thought.
  • Choose an emotion tag (optional).
  • Browse the archive, search, and filter.
  • Use “I relate” to acknowledge without turning it into a comment section.
