Terms & Conditions

Keep Drafts safe, anonymous, and respectful.

What you cannot post

  • Real names, school names, usernames/handles, phone numbers, emails, addresses, or identifying details
  • Threats, harassment, hate speech, or targeted bullying
  • Links or advertisements

How responses work

Drafts intentionally removes likes and comments. The only interaction is “I relate”, which acknowledges a post without turning it into a debate or popularity contest.

Important note

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or a trusted adult.

Privacy

Drafts does not ask for names or profiles. Still, you are responsible for keeping your post anonymous. If you include identifying details, your anonymity is not protected.
